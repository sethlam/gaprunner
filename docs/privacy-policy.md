# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 24, 2026

## Gap Runner

Gap Runner is a free mobile game. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

## Information We Collect

**We do not directly collect, store, or process any personal data.** The game does not require an account, login, or any personal information to play.

Game progress (scores, levels, settings) is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to any server.

## Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

### Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect and use data including:

- Advertising ID
- Device information (model, OS version)
- IP address
- App usage data

For more information, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) and [How Google uses data from apps](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites).

## Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Data Retention

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data to retain. All game data is stored locally on your device and can be cleared by uninstalling the app.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at [github.com/sethlam/gaprunner/issues](https://github.com/sethlam/gaprunner/issues).
